Ki Hajar Dewantara Library

An Oustanding school library that is very comfortable to visit

A Comfort School Library

This website recommends a school library that is comfortable and interesting because it can be visited by anyone. The library is called the KI Hajar Dewantara Library which was born at the same time as the founding of SMAN 70 Jakarta, on 5 October, 1981. The Ki Hajar Dewantara Library provides various services, including digital services, circulation services, reference services, literature services and children's services. This library has a reputation for the quality of service and completeness of its collections.

Display Services

The digital service available is an online catalog which contains information about all available collections. This library also provides e-books and e-journals which can be accessed via the Edoo platform.

Circulation Services

This service helps users in the process of borrowing, returning and extending the loan of library materials. This service also includes other administrative activities, such as member registration, identity verification, catalog management, transaction recording and loan timing.

Reference Services

The types of references available here include encyclopedias, maps, atlases, globes, dictionaries (Indonesian, English, German and French), bibliographies, catalogues, indexes, theses and other scientific works

Literature Services

In this service, the SMAN 70 Jakarta library provides a literature list or bibliography on various specific subjects that users might need. In essence, this service helps search for information that is relevant to the user's needs.

Children's Services

Children's services at the SMAN 70 Jakarta Library collaborate with the Public Library Working Group and the RW around the school by providing facilities in the form of children's tables and benches, as well as various children's reading materials that have been adapted to their tastes. This of course aims to improve children's literacy from an early age.
